# ➡️ Power Plant Energy Predictor

#### Build and end-to-end Deeplearning Artificial Neural Network using FNN (feedforward Neural Network) to predict the energy produce from the Power plant station dataset
### Project Description ⏬
##### Dataset contains 9570 rows and 4 columns (features)
### ⏬ Features :
#### AT: Ambient Temperature the temperature of the surrounding air (usually in °C). This affects the density of the air entering the gas turbine.
#### V: Exhaust Vacuum the pressure of the steam after it has passed through the turbine (usually in cm Hg).
#### AP: Ambient Pressure the atmospheric pressure at the plant's location (usually in millibar).
#### RH: Relative Humidity the percentage of moisture in the air.
#### PE: Net Hourly Electrical Energy Output this is your target variable (the $Y$ value you are trying to predict). It represents the actual electricity produced by the plant (usually in MW).

### ➡️ Model Evaluation :
##### Predicted 90%accuracy in both testing and training data
